Return Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

At TheWhyte Store, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help with our easy return process.

1. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In its original condition (unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached)
  • In the original packaging
  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Gift cards
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging

3. How to Initiate a Return

To request a refund, please write to use returns@thewhyte.store

4. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong item shipped, defective product). We recommend using a trackable shipping service as we cannot be responsible for lost return packages.

5. Refund Process

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item(s) and process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable.

6. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery. We will provide a prepaid return label and process a replacement or refund once we receive the returned item.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact our customer service team at returns@thewhyte.store or call us at +91-9074751798.
