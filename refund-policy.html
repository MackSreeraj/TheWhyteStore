Refund Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

At TheWhyte Store, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for receiving a refund for products purchased through our website.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Items must be returned within 15 days of the delivery date
  • Items must be in their original condition (unworn, unwashed, with all tags attached)
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Refundable"
  • Gift cards
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Personalized or custom-made items

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please write to use refunds@thewhyte.store

4. Refund Processing Time

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your payment method and financial institution.

5. Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. If the original payment method is not available, we may issue store credit at our discretion.

6. Shipping Costs

Original shipping fees are non-refundable, unless the return is due to our error. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except in cases where the return is due to our mistake (wrong item shipped, defective product, etc.).

7. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the expected time frame, please:

  1. Check your bank account or credit card statement
  2. Contact your credit card company or bank
  3. If you still haven't received your refund, contact our customer service

8. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

9. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will provide instructions for returning the item and will process a replacement or refund once we receive the returned item.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact our customer service team at refunds@thewhyte.store or call us at +91-9074751798.
